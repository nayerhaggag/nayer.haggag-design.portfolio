About Me

Hi, I'm Nayer Haggag, a sophomore in high school with a strong interest in engineering and design. I enjoy creating projects that combine creativity, functionality, and problem-solving. Through each project, I continue to develop my technical skills and explore innovative ways to solve real-world challenges.

Projects

Magnetic Whiteboard Organization Tool

Designed and built a magnetic organizer that securely holds whiteboard markers and a microfiber cloth, helping keep classroom supplies organized and easily accessible.

The product was developed to address the common issue of misplaced markers and cleaning materials in classrooms while maintaining a compact and functional design.

Solar-Powered Charger

As part of Project Shams, an initiative founded alongside upperclassman Aly Abo Ella, I designed and built portable solar-powered chargers.

The chargers were created to provide sustainable sources of electricity and were distributed to people without reliable access to power in Tunis Village.

Solar-Powered Power Bank

Through Project Shams, an initiative created alongside upperclassman Aly Abo Ella, I designed and built solar-powered power banks capable of storing energy collected from solar panels.

These devices were distributed to individuals without reliable access to electricity in Tunis Village, showing how renewable energy can support everyday needs.
