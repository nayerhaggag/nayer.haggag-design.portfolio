Projects

Product Design

Magnetic Whiteboard Organization Tool

I designed and built a magnetic organizer that securely holds whiteboard markers with a microfiber cloth, helping keep classroom supplies organized and easily accessible. The product addresses the common issue of unorganized whiteboard tools while maintaining a compact and functional design.

Project Shams

Solar-Powered Charger

As part of Project Shams, an initiative I founded alongside upperclassman Aly Abo Ella, we designed and built portable solar-powered chargers. These devices were created to provide sustainable sources of electricity and were distributed to under priveleged individuals without reliable access to power in Tunis Village.

Independent Project

Solar-Powered Power Bank

I built this solar-powered power bank separately in my free time as an improved version of my earlier solar charger. The project focused on storing solar energy for later use, making the design more practical, portable, and reliable for charging devices when sunlight is not available.
