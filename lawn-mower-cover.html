Lawn Mower Cover

Personal Project

The Problem

For my grandfather's birthday, I wanted to build him something useful rather than buy a gift. His garage had no dedicated space for his lawn mower, and it was constantly buried under clutter.

The Approach

I gave myself one constraint: build it entirely out of materials and tools already in the house. Using plywood and a roughly 3mm sheet of wood, I cut the pieces with a hand saw and a circular saw, then joined everything with wood glue and nails. The final design turns the unused vertical space above the mower into a simple, functional cover and storage structure.

The Result

My grandfather was thrilled with it. We don't live in the same country anymore — he's in the US and I'm in Egypt — but he still sends me pictures of it in use, which makes it one of my favorite projects to look back on.
