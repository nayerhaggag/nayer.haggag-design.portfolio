Magnetic Whiteboard Organization Tool

Product Design

I designed and built a magnetic organizer that securely holds whiteboard markers and a microfiber cloth, helping keep classroom supplies organized and easily accessible. The product addresses the common issue of misplaced writing tools while maintaining a compact and functional design.

Key Features

  • Magnetic attachment to whiteboards
  • Holds multiple markers securely
  • Dedicated microfiber cloth holder
  • Compact and functional design
