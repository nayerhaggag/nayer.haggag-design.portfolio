Solar-Powered Charger

Project Shams

As part of Project Shams, an initiative founded alongside upperclassman Aly Abo Ella, I designed and built portable solar-powered chargers. These devices were created to provide sustainable sources of electricity and were distributed to individuals without reliable access to power in Tunis Village.

Key Features

  • Portable and reliable solar-powered charging
  • Designed for under priveleged communities with limited access to electricity
  • Built as part of Project Shams, an initiative aimed to support under priveleged individuals through engineering
  • Distributed to individuals in Tunis Village, a rural area of Egypt without access to reliable electricity
